About Us

Built for
Impact

ATC Sports Complex is a premier multi-sporting facility in Truganina, Victoria. Designed to deliver scale, performance, and purpose in one place — our complex serves athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and community members of all levels.

From elite court hire to community fitness classes, we provide world-class facilities in a welcoming environment for everyone.

  • Five full-size basketball courts
  • Outdoor tracks & soccer field
  • Cricket training zones
  • Gender-segregated gyms (coming soon)
  • Tournament & event facility hire
  • Sports & Fitness programs
