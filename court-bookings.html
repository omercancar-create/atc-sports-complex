Simple Process

How to Book

Booking a court at ATC Sports Complex is straightforward. Contact us directly via phone or email to check availability and confirm your session.

01
Choose Your Sport

Select from basketball, tennis, volleyball, futsal, cricket, table tennis and more. Let us know your preferred sport and court type.

02
Check Availability

Contact us via phone on 0485 560 348 or email courthire@atc.vic.edu.au to check available time slots for your preferred date.

03
Confirm & Play

Confirm your booking and arrive ready to play. Our staff will have your court set up and ready for your session at the agreed time.
